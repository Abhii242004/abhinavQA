# Product Specifications

## Discount Rules
- The discount code **SAVE15** applies a **15%** discount.
- Invalid codes must trigger the message: *"Invalid discount code"*.

## Shipping Rules
- **Standard Shipping**: Free
- **Express Shipping**: $10 additional charge

## Pricing
- Wireless Mouse: $20
- Keyboard: $30
- USB-C Cable: $10

## Checkout Rules
- All form fields (Name, Email, Address) are required.
- Email must be in valid format.
- “Payment Successful!” should appear only if the form is valid.
